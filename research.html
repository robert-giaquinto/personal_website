Health Informatics

As a research assistant in the Institute of Health Informatics, I use natural language processing techniques on clinical notes.
These results can be used for:

  1. Data mining of patient's family and social history to search for correlations in their health.
  2. Quickly scanning doctors' patient history for possible interaction with specific diseases or infections.
  3. Automatically extracting relevant sections of doctor's notes to be shared with patients, creating a more transparent patient experience.

Air Pollution Modeling
